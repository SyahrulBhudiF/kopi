# James Hoffmann V60 Method

James Hoffmann's refined V60 technique focuses on even extraction through multiple small pours, creating a clean and balanced cup with excellent clarity.

## Equipment Needed

- Hario V60 dripper (size 02)
- V60 filters
- Gooseneck kettle
- Digital scale with timer
- Burr grinder

## Instructions

1. **Prep**: Heat water to 95°C. Rinse filter thoroughly and preheat V60 and server
2. **Grind**: 30g coffee, medium-fine grind (slightly finer than table salt)
3. **Bloom**: Create small well in grounds, pour 60g water in 10 seconds, wait 45 seconds total
4. **Pour 1**: Pour 60g more (120g total) in center, finish by 1:15
5. **Pour 2**: Pour 60g more (180g total) in slow spiral, finish by 1:45
6. **Pour 3**: Pour 60g more (240g total) in center, finish by 2:15
7. **Pour 4**: Pour 60g more (300g total) in slow spiral, finish by 2:45
8. **Pour 5**: Pour 60g more (360g total) in center, finish by 3:15
9. **Pour 6**: Pour 60g more (420g total) in slow spiral, finish by 3:45
10. **Final**: Pour 80g more (500g total) in center, finish by 4:15
11. **Drain**: Should finish dripping by 6:00

## Notes

- Maintain consistent pour rate and pattern
- Keep water level consistent between pours
- Adjust grind if total time is significantly off target
- This method works best with light to medium roasts
